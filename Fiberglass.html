Fiberglass Repair Services

Whether your boat needs a robust structural fix or a little cosmetic TLC, I'm here to help you sail smoothly. I don't just work with fiberglass—I tackle all sorts of composites, from Divinycel, Coosa Board, Starboard, and Starlite to even wood. Armed with top-notch products like polyester, vinylester, epoxy, expanding foam, and Awlgrip, I ensure every repair is as durable as it is stylish. And if you’re dreaming of custom features—be it a quirky fish box, a perfectly crafted hatch, or built-ins that make your boat truly one-of-a-kind—I've got you covered. Let’s make your boat not just seaworthy, but a real head-turner on the water!

  • Expert restoration with fiberglass, Divinycel, Coosa Board, Starboard, Starlite, nidacore, wood, and more
