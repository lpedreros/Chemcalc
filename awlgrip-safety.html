Awlgrip Topcoats Safety Guidelines

Handling and Storage

Use in well-ventilated areas, avoid skin and eye contact, and store in a cool, dry place.

First Aid Measures

For skin contact, wash with soap and water. In case of eye contact, rinse thoroughly with water for several minutes.

Exposure Controls/Personal Protection

Wear protective gloves, clothing, and eye/face protection. Ensure adequate ventilation at all times.

Fire Fighting Measures

Use alcohol-resistant foam, CO2, dry chemical powders, or water spray to extinguish fires. Avoid using excessive water.

Accidental Release Measures

Contain any spills and use non-combustible absorbent materials to collect the spill for proper disposal.

Special Notes

Dispose of any unused product and empty containers in accordance with local environmental regulations.
