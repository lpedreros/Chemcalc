Awlgrip Topcoats Safety Guidelines

Handling and Storage

Use in well-ventilated areas, avoid skin and eye contact, and store in a cool, dry place.

First Aid Measures

For skin contact, wash with soap and water. In case of eye contact, rinse with water for several minutes.

Exposure Controls/Personal Protection

Wear protective gloves, clothing, and eye/face protection. Ensure adequate ventilation.

Fire Fighting Measures

Use alcohol-resistant foam, CO2, powders, or water spray for extinguishing fires.

Accidental Release Measures

Contain spillage, and collect with non-combustible, absorbent material.

Special Notes

Dispose of contents/container in accordance with local regulations.
