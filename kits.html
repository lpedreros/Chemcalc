Our Custom Kit Options

At ChemCalc, I offer three convenient options to help you get the materials you need for your marine repairs:

  1. Custom Link List: For a modest fee (around $50), I'll create a personalized list of affiliate links tailored to your repair project. You'll receive a comprehensive list of products I recommend, so you can easily purchase exactly what you need.
  2. Assembled Kit Service: For a higher fee, I can personally assemble a complete repair kit for you. With this service, you'll pay the retail price for all the necessary materials, tools, and supplies, plus shipping fees. In addition, there is a separate assembly fee (typically in the range of $100–$150, depending on the complexity of the kit) for gathering and packaging everything together for your convenience.
  3. DIY Purchase: You can also browse our kits page and use the provided affiliate links to purchase the materials yourself. Using these links not only makes it easy to get the items you need but also supports ChemCalc at no extra cost to you.

This way, whether you want a fully curated kit delivered to your door or prefer to pick up the pieces yourself using the affiliate links, there’s an option that fits your needs.
