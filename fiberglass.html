Quality Repairs, Expert Guidance & Recurring Training

With years of hands-on experience, I not only deliver top-quality structural and cosmetic repairs on boats using fiberglass, Divinycel, Coosa Board, Starboard, Starlite, nidacore, wood, and more—but I also teach you how to do it yourself. Whether you're interested in live, hands-on training sessions, tele-guiding, or comprehensive premium courses, I offer personalized guidance to help you master every repair project with confidence.
