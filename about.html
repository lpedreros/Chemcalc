About ChemCalc

Welcome to ChemCalc – the online resource powered by Think & Engage, a company born from a passion for marine repair and a commitment to excellence. With years of hands-on experience transforming neglected boats into seaworthy masterpieces, I’ve mastered the art and science of repairs using materials like fiberglass, Divinycel, Coosa Board, Starboard, Starlite, nidacore, wood, and more.

ChemCalc isn’t just about repair services—it’s about sharing knowledge. I provide advanced chemical calculators, step-by-step tutorials, and personalized training sessions designed to empower both marine professionals and DIY enthusiasts. My goal is to guide you through every project with precision and confidence.

What sets Think & Engage apart is a relentless focus on quality and education. Whether you’re seeking practical tools for your next repair project or eager to learn the intricacies of marine restoration, ChemCalc is your trusted platform. Join me on this journey as we blend expert craftsmanship with innovative techniques to achieve lasting results.
