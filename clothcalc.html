    Tips & Safety

    Always work in a well-ventilated area and wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) such as gloves, eye protection, and a respirator when working with resins and solvents.

    Refer to the specific Technical Data Sheet (TDS) and Safety Data Sheet (SDS) for the materials you are using for detailed handling instructions and safety precautions.

    Mix resin and hardener/catalyst thoroughly according to manufacturer's instructions. Improper mixing can lead to poor curing.

    Apply resin evenly to saturate the material. Avoid pooling or dry spots.

    Working time is affected by temperature and batch size. Larger batches and higher temperatures will reduce working time.
