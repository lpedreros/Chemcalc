Our Custom Kit Options

At ChemCalc, I offer three convenient options to help you get the materials you need for your marine repairs:

  1. Custom Link List: For a modest fee (around $50), I'll create a personalized list of affiliate links tailored to your repair project.
  2. Assembled Kit Service: For a higher fee, I can personally assemble a complete repair kit for you. You'll pay the retail price for all materials plus shipping fees, plus a separate assembly fee (typically $100–$150).
  3. DIY Purchase: Browse our kits page and use the provided affiliate links to purchase the materials yourself. This supports ChemCalc at no extra cost.

This way, whether you want a fully curated kit delivered to your door or prefer to pick up the pieces yourself, there’s an option that fits your needs.
